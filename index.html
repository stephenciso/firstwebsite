Self-care 101

Some easy self-care tips I have are the following:

  • Take a nice, hot bath.
  • Walk your pet...or just take a walk by yourself.
  • Learn a new recipe from your childhood.
  • Do a facemask.
  • Put on a guilty pleasure show...mine is 90 Day Fiance

Hobbies 101

One of the weirdest things about being in quarantine is having so much time. It really made me realize how much a typical American life requires you to always be on the move. With more time on my hands, I've been really appreciating my hobbies I was not able to do. Some hobbies I've been rotating are: gardening, video games, reading, learning how to cook, and organizing my life.
